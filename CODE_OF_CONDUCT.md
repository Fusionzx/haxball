# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community.
Be constructive, assume good intent, and focus feedback on the work rather than
the person.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include harassment, discriminatory language,
personal attacks, deliberate disruption, and publishing another person's
private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the project maintainer through
[GitHub](https://github.com/Fusionzx). Reports will be reviewed promptly and
handled with discretion. Maintainers may remove content or restrict
participation when needed to protect the community.
